# multi-agent-startup-evaluator
Multi-Agent Debate &amp; Decision System for Early-Stage Startup Evaluation

Early-stage startup evaluation is inherently uncertain, multi-dimensional, and adversarial. Investors must assess technical feasibility, market demand, financial viability, and regulatory risks with incomplete information. Traditional evaluation relies on human investment committees where experts debate different perspectives before reaching consensus. However, current AI tools typically generate single-agent assessments, lacking structured disagreement, role specialization, and transparent reasoning.

We propose a Multi-Agent Debate & Decision System that simulates an AI investment committee. Specialized agents (Market Analyst, Technical Lead, Finance/VC, Product Lead, Risk/Legal, and Skeptic) argue structured perspectives before a Moderator synthesizes a final decision: Go, Pivot, or No-Go, along with ranked risks and actionable de-risking experiments.
